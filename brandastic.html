01 — Business Overview📊 OVERVIEW

Orange County's Digital Marketing Agency

Brandastic is an award-winning, full-service digital marketing agency headquartered in Costa Mesa, CA. Founded in 2010, we've served 450+ clients across healthcare, education, fashion, dental, and enterprise — including brands like RICOH, Orangetheory Fitness, HyperX, and KIA Motors.

Our services span SEO, PPC (Google Ads & Meta), social media marketing, web development, branding, and content marketing. With 15+ years of continuous operation and 40+ five-star Google reviews, the brand is strong — but our own website's SEO hasn't received the same attention we give clients.

Strengths
  • ✦ 15+ years, 450+ clients served
  • ✦ 5.0★ Google rating (40+ reviews)
  • ✦ Strong local brand recognition in OC
  • ✦ Active blog with SEO-focused content
Key Weaknesses
  • ⚠ Organic traffic well below competitor levels
  • ⚠ No active Google Ads for own brand
  • ⚠ Social media presence could be stronger
  • ⚠ Site speed needs optimization

10 — Critical Findings🚀 STRATEGY

Top 5 Issues

The most impactful problems holding back our own digital growth, ranked by severity.

1
Traffic Gap vs Competitors
With 1,206 monthly visits vs Power Digital's 24,683 and Ignite's 13,372, we're leaving massive lead potential on the table. Our domain has the authority — we just need the content volume.
2
Zero Self-Invested Google Ads
We manage client ad campaigns daily but invest $0 in our own lead generation via Google Ads. This is the classic "cobbler's children have no shoes" problem.
3
Aging Blog Content Portfolio
Our top-ranking posts target outdated topics (Alexa Rank, LSI SEO). We need fresh content targeting current trends like AI marketing, AEO, and emerging search patterns.
4
Site Speed & Technical Debt
Heavy WordPress theme with multiple plugins hurts mobile performance. Despite Cloudflare APO + WP Rocket, there's room for significant optimization.
5
Social Media Below Agency Standards
941 LinkedIn followers and inconsistent posting across platforms doesn't reflect our 15-year expertise. Our social presence should be a showcase, not an afterthought.
