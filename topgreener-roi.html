Why 5x+ ROAS is Achievable

Ultra-Low CPCs

TopGreener keywords avg $0.36–$0.72 CPC vs $2–5+ for most e-commerce. More clicks per dollar = more conversions.

High-Intent Searches

"USB outlet", "GFCI receptacle", "NEMA 6-20" — these are buyers searching for exactly what TopGreener sells.

Google Shopping Advantage

Physical products with images in the $15–35 range perform extremely well on Shopping ads. Typically 6–10x ROAS.

Merchant Center Ready

Shopify product feed → Google Merchant Center is a plug-and-play setup. Product data is already structured.

Key Challenges

1. Conversion Rate Optimization

TopGreener.com needs to convert at 4%+ for this model to work. Current site conversion rate is unknown — if it's below 2%, we need to fix the site before scaling ads.

Fix: Product page optimization, reviews/social proof, simplified checkout, bundle offers, exit-intent popups
KPI: Site Conversion Rate → Target 4%+

2. Average Order Value

Individual products range $8–$45. Bundling products, multi-packs, and upsells will maximize AOV and strengthen ROAS performance.

Fix: Product bundles ("Complete outlet kit"), volume discounts, free shipping threshold ($50+), post-purchase upsells
KPI: Average Order Value → Target $35+

3. Amazon Cannibalization

TopGreener has a strong Amazon presence. Shoppers may see our Google ad → then buy on Amazon instead. DTC must offer a reason to buy direct.

Fix: DTC-exclusive bundles, loyalty program, faster shipping, warranty registration only on topgreener.com
KPI: DTC vs Amazon Revenue Split

4. Click-Through Rate on Shopping

Product images, titles, pricing, and reviews all impact Shopping ad CTR. Poor product feed = low CTR = wasted impressions.

Fix: Optimized product feed (titles with keywords), high-quality images, competitive pricing, Google seller ratings
KPI: Shopping CTR → Target 2%+

Discussion Notes for Client Meeting

Brandastic Questions for TopGreener

  1. Current DTC conversion rate? — If below 2%, site optimization comes before ad spend scaling
  2. Current AOV on topgreener.com? — If below $25, we need bundle strategy first
  3. Amazon vs DTC revenue split? — What % of sales currently come through the website?
  4. Existing Google Merchant Center? — Is product feed already set up, or starting from scratch?
  5. Top-selling products on Amazon? — We'll prioritize these in Shopping campaigns
  6. Margin by product category? — Some categories may not support paid acquisition at low AOV
  7. Shipping policy? — Free shipping threshold drives AOV. Recommend $50+ free shipping
  8. Review collection? — Google seller ratings require 100+ reviews. Do they have a review strategy?

Brandastic Recommendations

  1. Month 1: Merchant Center setup, product feed optimization, Shopping campaign launch (80/20 split)
  2. Month 2: Optimize bids by product group performance, A/B test product titles, add negative keywords
  3. Month 3: Scale winners, pause underperformers, introduce remarketing via Meta ($1,750/mo add-on)
  4. Month 4+: Email automation (cart abandonment, browse abandonment, post-purchase), loyalty program
  5. Ongoing: Monthly reporting on all KPIs above, weekly bid adjustments, quarterly strategy reviews

Key Assumptions in This Model

  • CPC data sourced from SEMrush (May 2026) — actual CPCs may vary ±20%
  • Conversion rates assume optimized product pages and Shopping feed
  • AOV assumes bundle/multi-pack strategy is implemented
  • ROAS projections are for Google Ads only — Meta and Email are additive
  • Seasonal factors (Q4 holiday, contractor season) not modeled — expect 20-40% lift in Q4
  • Competitor ad activity may increase CPCs over time
