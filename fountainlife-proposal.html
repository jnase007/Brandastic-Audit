  • •CEO, Founder, Managing Director, Partner, C-Suite

Creative Strategy
  • •"Early detection saved my life" testimonial videos
  • •Before/after health transformation stories
  • •Diamandis authority clips → credibility ads
  • •Carousel: "What 150GB of health data reveals"

Meta Lead Form Pre-Qualification

Add qualifying questions directly inside Meta's in-app lead forms — before leads hit your sales team. This is a simple change that can dramatically raise close rates.

Q: "What is your approximate household income?" Q: "Are you interested in memberships starting at $10K/yr?" Q: "What best describes your health goal?"

Result: Unqualified leads self-select out. Your sales team only speaks to people who said yes to the price point.
