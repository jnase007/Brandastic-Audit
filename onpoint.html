01 — Business Overview

Helping First-Gen Students Succeed

On Point for College is a Syracuse, NY-based nonprofit dedicated to helping first-generation students get into and through college. They provide hands-on guidance through the admissions process, financial aid applications, and college persistence.

Their mission is critical — but their digital presence isn't keeping pace with their impact. With heavy reliance on paid advertising and virtually no organic search strategy, they're leaving significant growth on the table.

Goals
  • ✦ Increase awareness & visibility
  • ✦ Drive donation growth
  • ✦ Expand programs nationwide
  • ✦ Reduce paid ad dependency
Key Weaknesses
  • ⚠ Zero technical SEO foundation
  • ⚠ $154K/mo paid ad dependency
  • ⚠ Not using Google Ad Grants
  • ⚠ Thin content & brand-only traffic

10 — Critical Findings

Top 5 Issues

The most impactful problems holding back your digital growth, ranked by severity.

1
Google Ad Grants Not Utilized
As an eligible nonprofit, you're leaving $120,000/year in free Google advertising on the table. This is the single biggest quick win available.
2
Zero Technical SEO Foundation
No schema markup, broken canonical tags, missing H1 structures, no XML sitemap optimization. The site is essentially invisible to search engine crawlers.
3
Destructive Cache Policy
Current caching configuration is actively hurting page speed and crawl efficiency, damaging your rankings potential.
4
Massive Paid Dependency — $154K/mo
With virtually no organic traffic engine, cutting ad spend would devastate visibility. This is an unsustainable model.
5
Brand-Only Organic Traffic
People only find you if they already know your name. You're completely invisible to the audiences searching for your exact services.
