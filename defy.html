01 — Business Overview

Premium Outdoor Eyewear
That Dares to Defy

DEFY is a Denver-based DTC eyewear brand engineered for outdoor adventurers — hikers, trail runners, cyclists, and thrill-seekers who demand performance without sacrificing style or sustainability.

Built on EMS Grivory Greenline technology (58% bio-based materials), DEFY frames are lightweight, hypoallergenic, and corrosion-resistant. HD polarized lenses deliver vibrant clarity with scratch and shatter resistance. Every pair includes a lifetime warranty and 30-day risk-free trial — a confidence play that signals product quality and commitment to customer experience.

Currently operating at $0 paid ad spend, DEFY is in the critical early-growth phase — brand identity is strong, the product story is compelling, and the digital foundation needs focused investment to compete with established DTC eyewear brands.

✦ Brand Strengths
  • ♻️ Genuine sustainability story (58% bio-based materials)
  • 🔒 Lifetime warranty + 30-day trial — strong trust signals
  • 💎 Premium positioning at accessible price ($69.99)
  • 🏔️ Clear niche: outdoor adventure community
  • 🛍️ Shopify DTC — full margin control, owned channel
⚠ Critical Gaps
  • 📉 Virtually zero organic traffic (just launched)
  • 💸 $0 paid media investment — no customer acquisition engine
  • 🔗 No backlink profile — zero domain authority built
  • 📱 No established social following yet
  • 📧 Email/SMS list likely near zero — no retention engine
📦 Product Line
  • 🕶️ ASPEN (DE104) — $69.99 — Flagship outdoor style
  • 🏔️ Canyon Collection — Adventure-focused line
  • 🌱 All frames: EMS Grivory Greenline, stainless steel + cellulose propionate
  • 🚚 Free shipping $55+ · Ships in 2 business days

11 — Growth Strategy

The DEFY Growth Engine

A coordinated 4-pillar strategy to take DEFY from $0 ad spend to a recognizable national outdoor eyewear brand.

🎯
Paid Media Launch
Start with Meta (Instagram/Facebook) Shopping ads targeting outdoor enthusiasts. Test creative with lifestyle imagery vs. product close-ups vs. UGC. Layer in Google Shopping & Brand terms. Scale winners to TikTok.
Goal: $2–3K/mo → 100+ orders/mo at <$25 CAC
🤝
Influencer Program
Launch a structured ambassador program. Seed product to 100+ nano & micro outdoor influencers. Create referral links for every creator. Build a community of authentic DEFY advocates who generate perpetual UGC.
Goal: 50+ pieces of UGC in 90 days
📧
Email & SMS
Build an owned audience from day 1. Launch Klaviyo flows: welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase sustainability story, review request at day 30. Add SMS for flash sales and new drops. Email list = zero-cost future revenue.
Goal: 1,000 subscribers in 60 days → 15% list revenue
⚡
CRO & Shopify
Every dollar spent on ads is amplified by higher conversion rate. Test landing pages for each ad campaign, optimize the product page (more reviews, better photos, trust signals front & center), add upsell for lens accessories.
Goal: 2.5%+ conversion rate (vs. 1.2% DTC average)

🌐 SEO Long-Term Engine

  • 1. Create category landing pages for each sport/activity (hiking, running, cycling, kayaking)
  • 2. Publish 4 SEO-optimized blog posts per month targeting outdoor eyewear keywords
  • 3. Build backlinks via outdoor media (GearJunkie, Outside Magazine, Trail Runner Mag) and PR outreach
  • 4. Implement full schema markup, improve Core Web Vitals, and submit to Google News
  • 5. Target competitor comparison keywords ("DEFY vs Goodr") for high-intent traffic

🏆 PR & Earned Media

  • • Pitch sustainability story to Outside Magazine, GearJunkie, Treehugger — "The Sunglasses Made from Plants"
  • • Submit for "Best New Gear" lists in outdoor media — huge SEO link value + brand credibility
  • • Partner with outdoor events (trail races, climbing comps) for product placement and sampling
  • • Amazon Handmade / REI Co-op marketplace listing for channel diversification
  • • HARO (Help a Reporter Out) responses for eyewear and sustainability topics
