02 — Technical SEO · 58/100

Technical Foundation: Partial

The basics are in place — SSL, mobile, sitemap. But the complete absence of structured data (schema markup) is a critical gap that costs rankings, rich snippets, and local visibility every single day.

What's Working

  • SSL certificate active — site fully HTTPS
  • robots.txt and sitemap.xml present
  • WP Rocket caching plugin active
  • Mobile viewport tag properly set
  • WordPress CMS — manageable and extensible

Critical Gaps

  • ZERO JSON-LD schema — no LocalBusiness, Dentist, or FAQPage markup
  • H4 tags misused as body copy (heading hierarchy broken)
  • 3 duplicate Google Site Verification codes in <head>
  • No CDN — site assets served from single origin
  • Duplicate URL: full-mouth-dental-implants vs full-mouth-dental-implants-las-vegas-nv
Schema markup is the single highest-leverage fix available. Google uses LocalBusiness + Dentist schema to power Maps rankings, knowledge panels, and rich snippets. Every competitor using schema has a structural advantage over jbwhitedds.com right now.
03 — On-Page SEO · 62/100

On-Page: Needs Keyword Strategy

The homepage is solid, but several key pages are missing keyword optimization — a problem because Google ranks individual pages, not whole websites.

Contact page title: "Contact Us: JB White, DDS"
No location, no service keywords. A wasted opportunity on a page Google may rank for "las vegas cosmetic dentist contact" type queries.
Fix: "Contact a Las Vegas Cosmetic Dentist | JB White, DDS"
Add keywords + location to signal relevance for local searchers.
Blog section title: just "Blog"
Blog category page has no keyword context. Google doesn't know what this blog is about.
Fix: "Dental Health Blog | JB White, DDS Las Vegas"
Descriptive titles help Google categorize and rank the entire blog section.

04 — Local SEO · 52/100

Local SEO: Major Gaps

For a dental practice, local SEO is everything. Patients search "dentist near me" and "cosmetic dentist las vegas" — and right now, jbwhitedds.com is not positioned to capture any of it.

Local Signals Present

  • Google Maps embed on contact page
  • Phone number visible in header
  • Listed on Yelp (citation exists)

Critical Local Failures

  • No LocalBusiness or Dentist schema markup
  • NAP (Name, Address, Phone) not in footer
  • No service area pages (Henderson, Summerlin)
  • No Review/AggregateRating schema
  • Phone format inconsistency: 702-823-3000 vs 702.823.3000

Local SEO components — current state vs. full optimization potential

NAP inconsistency hurts Local Pack rankings. Google cross-references your phone number across directories. Using two formats (dashes vs dots) creates conflicting citations that weaken local authority. Simple fix, big impact.

07 — Priority Roadmap

The Action Plan

Three phases, sequenced by impact. Each phase builds on the last — fix the foundation, then grow content, then amplify authority.

Month 1 — Foundation
Highest Priority
Fix What's Breaking Rankings Now
Actions
  • Implement LocalBusiness + Dentist JSON-LD schema
  • Add FAQPage schema to service pages
  • Add consistent NAP in footer (use one format)
  • Fully optimize Google Business Profile
  • Rewrite keyword-poor title tags (Contact, Blog, About)
  • Fix duplicate URL canonicalization issue on implants page
  • Remove duplicate Site Verification codes from <head>
Expected Impact

Schema markup alone can trigger rich snippets in Google Search and boost Local Pack rankings. Combined with a fully optimized GBP and consistent NAP, this phase typically shows measurable improvement within 4–8 weeks. These are the highest-leverage fixes available — and none require new content.

Months 2–3 — Content Expansion
High Priority
Build the Content That Captures Demand
Actions
  • Create dedicated All-on-4 implants page
  • Create Invisalign las vegas landing page
  • Add dental implant cost page (high commercial intent)
  • Add dental financing / payment options page
  • Expand About page to 1,000+ words featuring KOL credentials
  • Resolve composite veneers keyword cannibalization
  • Audit and refresh thin blog posts
Expected Impact

Invisalign (1,300/mo) and All-on-4 searches are high-intent, high-value. Creating these pages positions JB White for rankings that drive $10K–30K+ cases. An expanded About page with KOL credentials also builds E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) signals Google heavily weights for medical/dental sites.

Months 3–6 — Authority & Growth
Ongoing
Build the Authority Competitors Can't Match

Expected Impact

Neighborhood pages can capture "dentist near me" + location searches across the greater Las Vegas market. Backlinks from dental manufacturers and publications can dramatically improve Domain Authority within 6 months. A Google Ads campaign during the organic ramp-up ensures no search traffic opportunity is missed. Reviews directly influence Local Pack rankings and patient conversion.
