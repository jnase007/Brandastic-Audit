4. Findings (prioritized)

P0 Fix first

  1. Homepage media noise — YouTube endscreen/related-video chrome pollutes the experience and likely hurts LCP/CLS. Simplify hero, defer/lazy video, one primary CTA above the fold.
  2. Primary CTA consistency — Free Consultation / Work With Us / form / phone compete. Pick one primary conversion for paid/SEO paths and repeat it.
  3. Contact form friction — Keep reCAPTCHA, but measure start→submit; add click-to-call + calendar secondary on mobile.

P1 SEO / service strength

  1. Money page consistency — Every core service page needs unique H1, proof, FAQs, related work links, and one hard CTA.
  2. Portfolio → service internal links — Case studies should deep-link exact services/industries.
  3. Blog → money keyword bridging — Keep AI/SEO content, systematically support commercial intent.

P2 AI visibility / entity

  1. Entity clarity is already decent (locations, phone, portfolio, taxonomy). Confirm Organization schema + sameAs + keep AI facts page fresh.
  2. Package explicit proof stats in crawlable text (years, clients, verticals) — not logo walls alone.
