Opportunity

Nordic Sauna already has real AI presence — 3.36% share of voice (5th in the set) with a strong average answer position of 2.4 and 86.7% favorable sentiment — but sits well behind Klafs (11.5%), Finnleo (9.1%), Sun Home (8.4%) and Sunlighten (6.9%). From a business-driver view, Nordic Sauna is strongest exactly where margins and strategic accounts live: custom design, commercial-grade reliability, multi-site projects, and turnkey design-to-install. But it's underexposed in the AI content where household buyers do early research — answers on 'best outdoor/backyard saunas,' 'most durable residential saunas,' or 'best value' largely spotlight brands with modular kits and DTC models that resonate with first-time buyers and lifestyle media. Notably, when AI does cite Nordic Sauna's own Custom Sauna Builder page, it does so 8/8 times at 96% favorable — the authority is there; the coverage breadth is not.

Recommendations
  • Clarify a dual-track go-to-market story in AI: one for trade and pro-specifiers, one for aspirational homeowners — so AI can match Nordic Sauna to the right buyer intent.
  • For pros, double down on technical guides, spec sheets, and commercial case studies that are easy for AI to cite (the sources it already leans on: Forbes Vetted, Jacuzzi's manufacturer guide, GarageGymReviews).
  • For homeowners, create approachable narratives and tools — budget tiers, good/better/best comparisons, lifetime-cost calculators — that answer 'is it worth stepping up from a kit?' in concrete terms.
  • Win back sentiment and SOV with architect/owner testimonials and comparison-style content placed in the exact buyer guides and expert blogs AI Mode already relies on (youtube.com, sunhomesaunas.com references, havenofheat.com, funoutdoorliving.com).
