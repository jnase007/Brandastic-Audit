Business Overview

Paragon Implant Company was founded in 2022 by Dr. Gerald Niznick, DMD, MSD — a prosthodontist with 43 years in the dental implant industry and 35 US patents. FDA clearance received November 2025 — on the market for 6-7 months. The GEN5 system features a machined collar, anodized surface, and unique vertical flexibility (supracrestal placement) — currently the only FDA-cleared option of its kind. At ~$125 per implant vs $600 for major competitors, Paragon offers a 5x pricing advantage with reduced risk of peri-implantitis.

Key Challenges

Reaching Younger Dentists

Younger dental professionals are often debt-burdened and working in DSOs (Dental Service Organizations). Different messaging and channels needed vs independent practice owners.

Email Performance

Weekly emails to 95K contacts but performance needs improvement. Segmentation between buyers, trial users, and unconverted contacts is critical.

Website & Brand Appeal

Current WordPress/WooCommerce site needs updates to appeal to younger dentists while respecting Dr. Niznick's preferences. Brand refresh needed.

Marketing Talent Gap

Difficulty hiring experienced marketing talent in Las Vegas. Josie seeks an embedded marketing partnership to upskill internal staff, not pure outsourcing.

Regulatory vs Speed

FDA-cleared only since Nov 2025. Balancing regulatory compliance timelines with aggressive marketing execution for $15M goal.

Journal Ads Too Expensive

Traditional dental journal ads cost ~$5K each with limited trackability. Digital channels offer better ROI and targeting.
