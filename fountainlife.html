01 — Business Overview

The Future of Longevity

Fountain Life is a premium longevity and healthspan clinic backed by Peter Diamandis. With locations in Dallas, Orlando, Naples, New York, and Houston, they offer full-body scans, AI-powered diagnostics, and concierge health memberships.

Despite a powerful brand and celebrity founder, 90%+ of their organic traffic comes from branded searches — a massive vulnerability. The longevity market is exploding, and they're missing 28K+ monthly searches from high-intent prospects.

Goals

  • ✦ Dominate longevity search landscape
  • ✦ Drive membership signups across 5 locations
Key Weaknesses

10 — Critical Findings

Top 5 Issues

The most impactful problems holding Fountain Life back from digital dominance.

1
90%+ Branded Traffic = Massive Vulnerability
Your entire organic presence depends on people already knowing your name. If a competitor captures the "longevity clinic" narrative, your traffic collapses.
2
Blog Dead Since September 2024
Eight months with zero content signals stagnation to Google. Fresh content is the #1 driver of non-branded organic growth — and you've stopped.
3
Weak Schema Markup on a Medical Site
MedicalBusiness, LocalBusiness, FAQ, and Review schema are table stakes for health sites. You're missing all of them, costing you rich snippets and trust.
4
28K+ Monthly Searches Uncontested
High-intent terms like "longevity clinic near me," "executive health screening," and "full body health assessment" drive your ideal patients — and you're invisible.
5
Location Pages Not Ranking Locally
Five premium locations in major metros, but none of them rank for local search terms. Each location is a missed opportunity for qualified leads.
