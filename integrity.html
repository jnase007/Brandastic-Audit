🔍
Screaming Frog crawl reveals 133 pages with missing titles and 176 with missing meta descriptions — these are invisible to search engines.

On-Page Issues

● 133 missing page titles (14.2%) — pages without titles don't appear properly in search results
● 176 missing meta descriptions (18.8%) — Google generates random snippets instead
● 133 missing H1 tags (14.2%) — search engines can't determine page topics
● 11 duplicate title groups — e.g. 7× "Training Resources Center", 7× "News | Integrity Solutions"
● 381 titles too long (40.7% over 60 chars) — getting truncated in search results
● 103 descriptions too long (>160 chars) + 68 too short (<70 chars)

Technical Issues

● 290 non-indexable pages (31%) — nearly a third of the site is hidden from Google
● 131 redirect chains (301s) — slowing crawl efficiency and diluting link equity
● 2 internal + 31 external 404s — broken links hurt user experience and crawl budget
● 73 pages returning 403 Forbidden — blocking both users and search engines
● Broken external links — LinkedIn profiles, BCG articles, old internal URLs returning errors
● 655 images — need alt text audit for accessibility and image SEO
