01 — Business Overview

Luxury Permanent Florals

A Bloomery is a luxury faux floral brand on Shopify, offering real-touch arrangements so lifelike that guests think they're real. Founded by Jannah Rostoum, the brand began in wedding florals and has evolved into a direct-to-consumer home décor destination.

With arrangements priced around $100–110, A Bloomery sits in the premium home décor space — but the biggest challenge today is that the website doesn't instantly communicate that these are faux (permanent) flowers, not fresh blooms.

This proposal outlines a comprehensive digital growth strategy to clarify the brand message, drive targeted traffic through Meta ads, and build a retention engine with Klaviyo email automation.

Brand Snapshot
  • ✦ Luxury real-touch faux floral arrangements
  • ✦ Shopify e-commerce — DTC model
  • ✦ Origin: wedding florals → home décor
  • ✦ ~$100–110 average product price
Key Challenge
  • ⚠ Site doesn't clearly communicate flowers are FAUX
  • ⚠ Brand story & narrative need strengthening
  • ⚠ No email automation or retention flows
  • ⚠ Meta ads running but no in-app lead gen or first-time buyer offer
Opportunity
  • ✦ Premium product with strong visual appeal
  • ✦ Wedding client base = warm audience
  • ✦ Massive seasonal demand (Mother's Day, etc.)
  • ✦ Interior designer market untapped
02 — Key Recommendations

What We'd Change

Based on our discovery call, here are the three pillars of immediate action to accelerate A Bloomery's growth.

🌐
Website & Messaging
  • Make it INSTANTLY clear flowers are faux/permanent
  • Strengthen Jannah's personal story & brand narrative
  • Add urgency elements (limited stock, seasonal)
  • Social proof: reviews, UGC photos, trust badges
  • Conversion-focused product page optimization
📱
Meta Ads Optimization
  • Already running influencer video ads — strong foundation ✅
  • Add in-app lead gen forms with first-time buyer discount → Klaviyo
  • Build a CTA funnel: capture emails before asking for the sale
  • Test new audiences: interior designers, lookalikes
  • Retargeting flows for cart abandoners
  • Creative testing framework to prevent ad fatigue
📧
Email Marketing (Klaviyo)
  • Welcome series: brand story + first purchase incentive
  • Abandoned cart recovery flows
  • Post-purchase: care tips, cross-sell, review request
  • Seasonal drop campaigns (holidays, new collections)
  • VIP/loyalty segmentation for repeat buyers
