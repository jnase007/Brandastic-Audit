Welcome to Brandastic

To get started, we need access to your marketing platforms. Follow the steps below to share credentials securely — it only takes a few minutes.

Share access to: seo@brandastic.com
1

Google Ads

Share your Google Ads account so we can manage your campaigns

  1. a Visit Google Ads and sign into your account
  2. b Select your Ads account and send us your Google Ads ID Number (top left corner)
  3. c Watch for an account access request email from us
  4. d To manually check: go to Admin → Access and Security → Managers tab and accept the manager link request
2

Google Tag Manager

Share container access so we can manage tracking and tags

  1. a Visit Google Tag Manager and sign in
  2. b Find your account and click the ⚙️ settings icon
  3. c Click "Add users" and share with seo@brandastic.com
  4. d Select Administrator access under Account permissions and click Invite
3

Google Analytics

Share analytics access so we can track and optimize performance

  1. a Visit Google Analytics and go to the ⚙️ Admin panel (lower left)
  2. b Select Account Access Management under the Account column
  3. c Click + Add users and enter seo@brandastic.com
  4. d Select Administrator access and click Add
4

Google Search Console

Share search performance data so we can optimize your SEO

  1. a Visit Google Search Console
  2. b Click the ⚙️ Settings gear icon in the left menu
  3. c Under "Users and permissions", click "Add user"
  4. d Enter seo@brandastic.com and select Full permission level
  5. e Click Add and notify us when complete

That's it!

Once you've shared access to all four platforms, let us know and we'll get started on your campaigns. If you need help with any step, reach out to your account manager.
