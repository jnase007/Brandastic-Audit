Client Onboarding

Complete each step to share access to your marketing platforms. Click each tab, follow the instructions, and mark it done.

1

Google Ads

Share your Ads account for campaign management

a

Visit Google Ads

Go to business.google.com/in/google-ads and sign in

b

Send us your Ads ID

Find the Google Ads ID Number in the top left corner and send it to us

c

Accept our access request

Go to Admin → Access and Security → Managers tab and accept the manager link request

2

Google Tag Manager

Share container access for tracking management

a

Visit Tag Manager

Go to tagmanager.google.com and sign in

b

Open settings & add user

Click the ⚙️ icon on your account, then "Add users"

c

Share with Administrator access

Add seo@brandastic.com → check Administrator → click Invite

3

Google Analytics

Share analytics for performance tracking

a

Visit Google Analytics

Go to analytics.google.com → ⚙️ Admin panel (lower left)

b

Go to Account Access Management

Under the Account column, select Account Access Management

c

Add us as Administrator

Click + Add users → enter seo@brandastic.com → select Administrator → click Add

4

Google Search Console

Share search data for SEO optimization

a

Visit Search Console

Go to search.google.com/search-console

b

Open Users and Permissions

Click ⚙️ Settings → Users and permissions → Add user

c

Add with Full permission

Enter seo@brandastic.com → select Full permission → click Add

5

Meta Business Suite / Facebook Ads

Share ad account access for Facebook & Instagram campaigns

a

Visit Meta Business Suite

Go to business.facebook.com/settings and sign in

b

Go to People

Navigate to Settings → People and click "Add People"

c

Add our email with Admin access

Enter seo@brandastic.com → select Full Control / Admin → click Invite

d

Share Ad Account access

Go to Ad Accounts → select your account → Add People → add seo@brandastic.com with Manage Campaigns permission

e

Share Meta Pixel

Go to Data Sources → Pixels → select your pixel → Add People → add seo@brandastic.com

6

Instagram Business

Connect Instagram for social media management

a

Ensure Instagram is a Business or Creator account

Go to Instagram → Settings → Account → switch to Professional Account if not already

b

Connect to Facebook Page

Make sure your Instagram is connected to your Facebook Business Page in Meta Business Suite

c

Grant access via Meta Business Suite

In Business Settings → Instagram Accounts → Add People → add seo@brandastic.com

7

Website Access

Share CMS or hosting access for landing pages and tracking setup

a

WordPress (if applicable)

Create an Administrator account for seo@brandastic.com under Users → Add New

b

Shopify (if applicable)

Go to Settings → Users and Permissions → Add Staff → enter seo@brandastic.com with full permissions

c

Other CMS / Hosting

For Wix, Squarespace, Webflow, or custom sites — email us your platform and we'll send specific instructions

d

Domain / DNS access (optional)

If we need to set up subdomains or verify ownership, share your domain registrar login (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.)

✓

All Done!

You've shared access to all four platforms. We'll confirm everything on our end and reach out within 24 hours to get started.
