01 — Business Overview📊 OVERVIEW

Two Brands, One Opportunity

TopGreener and Lider Electric design and sell smart electrical products — USB outlets, surge protectors, dimmers, NEMA receptacles, and smart-home devices — with a powerful Amazon presence and a growing direct-to-consumer footprint.

The foundation is strong: 5,000+ combined organic keywords and dozens of #1 product rankings. But there's a glaring gap — zero paid advertising across either brand, with a $6K/month budget sitting unused. Every product search a competitor is buying is a sale you're not capturing.

Goals
  • ✦ Launch paid acquisition (Google + Meta)
  • ✦ Drive DTC sales off Amazon dependency
  • ✦ Differentiate Lider (premium) vs TopGreener (value)
  • ✦ Capture product-search demand
Key Weaknesses
  • ⚠ $0 ad spend — no Google or Meta ads
  • ⚠ No Google Shopping for an e-com brand
  • ⚠ No retargeting / Meta Pixel
  • ⚠ Thin content — no blog or buying guides

11 — Critical Findings🚀 STRATEGY

Top 5 Findings

The most impactful items shaping your growth — ranked by opportunity and urgency.

1
Zero Paid Advertising — $6K Budget Unused
Neither brand runs Google or Meta ads. With a catalog already ranking #1 organically, launching paid is the fastest, lowest-risk revenue lever available — and the budget is already approved.
2
No Google Shopping for an E-Commerce Brand
Product carousels drive the majority of e-com clicks. With no Shopping feed, competitors and retailers capture buyers searching your exact products.
3
No Retargeting Infrastructure
No Meta Pixel or Google remarketing means every site visitor who doesn't convert is lost. Retargeting is the cheapest, highest-ROAS paid tactic — and it's not running.
4
Lider Electric Lacks Differentiation
Lider trails TopGreener 1,065 to 4,060 keywords and leans on the generic "lider" term. Without clear premium positioning, the two brands cannibalize instead of complement.
5
Amazon-First — Strong Channel, Weak DTC
A 70/100 Amazon channel is a real asset, but website conversions trail. Optimizing Amazon and building DTC funnels improves margins and captures first-party data.
